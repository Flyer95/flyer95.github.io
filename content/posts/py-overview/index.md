# A New Beginning

Originally, I started posting “devlog-like” posts on a Discord server for the game. However, as time has gone on, leaving all of my development logs and messages on a single platform I don’t have complete control over hasn’t seemed like a good idea. I would hate for all the development notes and such to be locked on Discord or worse be lost if the server or Discord itself goes away. Going forward, everything Project Yukon related on the game will be stored here. Other kinds of posts (YouTube videos and stuff hosted on other sites) will have their own posts here so everything can be found in one place.

These first few posts will be re-posts of the Discord stuff with better focus and more depth and more space than Discord would allow.

Now, let’s talk about Project Yukon…

## What is Project Yukon?

Project Yukon is a third-person adventure that follows Carter, a young archeologist trying to unravel the mysteries surrounding an abandoned ancient civilization with help from an oddly curious fox. The mechanics involve the player and the, who Carter names Juniper, working together to solve puzzles and traverse the world. This unique mechanic allows for equally unique puzzle design and choices the player can make in relation to Juniper’s actions. It also doubles as a way to have the player (and carter) to bond with their companion as the game unfolds.

After 4 years of off-and-on work, I have a completed prototype and a clearer vision for the gameplay loop, story, and narrative for the game. That might sound like a long time and it is, but I think the Project Yukon that’s in development now wouldn’t be what it is without those 4 years.

I’m excited to continue to work on it and get more work done on it. And I hope you’re excited to follow along with me.
