### *Collaborative project released on January 2026.*

Transfusion is a VR hack and slash game that I worked on with the Transfusion Group. I was responsible for the game's soundtrack, as well as modeling and texturing several of the weapons in the game. I also worked on several marketing and art assets for the game, such as the gameplay trailer and achievement icons. In addition, I worked on the narrative for the story mode.
