### *Solo Project. Created in 2025.*

Light In The Dark is an FPS Horror game where you retrieve and upload hard drives that have been left on an abandoned spaceship. I did the modeling for everything other than the railgun model, using texture compression and post-processing for a PSX look. I also did all of the programing and wrote the music for the game.
